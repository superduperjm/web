Piano Inflatable Pool

Industrial Design / 2026

Project Background

The project aimed to redefine the traditional inflatable pool by incorporating musical elements. Designed specifically for young families, this pool combines summer cooling with an interactive "piano key" aesthetic to stimulate children's visual and tactile senses.

Challenges

Balancing the complex "keyboard" structure with the safety requirements of inflatable products was the primary challenge. We needed to ensure that the raised "keys" were comfortable and didn't compromise the pool's structural integrity.

Solution

By using multi-chamber inflation technology, we created independent air cushions for each key area. This not only provided a distinct visual effect but also enhanced the overall durability of the pool. The black and white contrast creates a striking presence in any backyard.
