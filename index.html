About Me

Hello, I'm Judy Min.

I am a designer passionate about exploring the future. From Arizona State University to Parsons School of Design in New York, my design journey has always revolved around a single core mission: creating designs that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also possess commercial vitality.

With a dual background in Industrial Design and Interaction Design, I navigate seamlessly between physical products and digital experiences. I view my role as more than just a designer; I am a "translator of the future"—transforming emerging trends into products that people truly need.

If you are looking for a design partner who combines creative energy with strategic business thinking, I'd love to connect!
